
Mix:
    1 c corn meal = 158 g 
    1 c ap flour = 130 g
    ¼ c (or less) sugar - 2 Tbs = 23 g 
    1 Tbs baking powder  - remove
    1 tsp salt - 4 g 

Whisk:
    1 egg = 40 g
    ⅓ c grease
    1 c milk / buttermilk / cream = 225g 

Pour liquids into solids, beat for a few minutes, raise a few hours.
Grease pan, scrape batter into pan, raise ~30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400°

Bake 30 minutes.
