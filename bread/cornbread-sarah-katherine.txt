Preheat oven and 8×8 pan to 400°

Mix:
    1 c corn meal
    1 c ap flour
    ¼ c (or less) sugar
    1 Tbs baking powder
    1 tsp salt

Whisk:
    1 egg
    ⅓ c grease
    1 c milk / buttermilk / cream

Quickly mix wet and dry ingredients.
Remove pan from oven, grease it, add batter, return to oven for 30 minutes.
