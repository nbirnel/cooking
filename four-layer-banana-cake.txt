Preheat oven to 350°F.
Cream:
    3 c sugar
    1 c shortening
Add, one by one:
    4 eggs
Add:
    3 bananas in chunks
Sift together:
    4 c cake flour
    1½ tsp tsp baking soda
    1½ tsp tsp baking powder
    1 tsp salt
and add to creamed ingredients alternately with:
    1¼ c buttermilk
Fold in:
    ½ c nut meats
Pour into 4 cake pans lined with wax paper.
Bake for 30 minutes.
