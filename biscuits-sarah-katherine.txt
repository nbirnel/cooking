Preaheat oven to 425°F
Mix:
    2 c ap flour
    ½ tsp salt
    4 tsp baking powder
   (½ tsp cream of tartar)
    1 Tbs (or less) sugar
Cut in:
    ½ c grease
Quickly mix in:
    ⅔ c milk
until barely combined. 
Turn out and knead no more than 10 times,
roll to desired thickness,
cut out,
and bake 15 - 20 minutes.

May be frozen before baking.
